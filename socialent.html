TEACH A SCHOOL


This is an initiative in collaboration with TEAM EVEREST INDIA, a voluntary group, that aims at inculcating entrepreneurial and other live skills in under privileged students and thereby motivating the young minds to DREAM BIG, to overcome their fears and step up for a better future.
Reach out to the students from various strata of life (including physically disabled and special children) and instil social values and morals with a higher preference to motivational & educational projects.
To teach life skills including art, soft skills, basic computer skills, team building activities that in the long run will empower them to stand on their own to face the world.
Educational planning, scholarships for deserving students and career guidance for students from higher classes.




LIGHT A SCHOOL


Light A School, is one of SSN Lakshya’s signature event. Members of the E- Cell embark on a three hour journey visiting villages in the district of Thirutani, about 96kms away from Chennai, to meet underprevilidged children. These children have dreams just like us.Dreams of becoming engineers, doctors and lawyers but those are inhibited by lack of electricity, good teachers and even basic necessities like water. To help them, we provide them with solar lamps to help light up their lives so that the sunset is no longer detrimental to their dreams. Every man must decide whether he will walk in the light of creative altruism or in the darkness of destructive selfishness.
Watching these students study in light of the lamps, swept us with an overwhelming feeling. A strange cocktail of joy, sadness and hope. Joy- of being able to make a difference to people’s lives, sadness- for the children who live with nothing but home.
It makes you appreciate what you’ve got and moreover, instills a feeling of compassion, to reach out to the less fortunate.
